Sylveste pushes the frontier on three axes simultaneously: state-of-the-art autonomy, uncompromising quality, and relentless token efficiency. Most agent systems pick two. Sylveste refuses to choose, orchestrating discovery, design, review, testing, and shipping with discipline encoded in gates, review agents, and a learning loop that feeds outcome data back into model routing. The whole thing is open source, named after Revelation Space, and designed so each layer survives the replacement of everything above it.
