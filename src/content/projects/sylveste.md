Sylveste is an open-source platform that orchestrates the full software development lifecycle with interdisciplinary domain agents and a recursive learning loop that feeds outcome data back to autonomously improve model routing and token costs. Most agent systems treat autonomy, quality, and cost as a pick-two tradeoff; Sylveste treats all three as hard constraints and empowers the routing layer to figure out the rest. Sylveste's Intercore kernel has outlived two generations of OS tooling already, which is the real test of whether one's orchestration approach is more than just a miserable little pile of tokens.
