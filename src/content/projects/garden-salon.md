Garden Salon is the experience side of Sylveste's two-brand architecture. Where Sylveste provides infrastructure in a science-fiction register, Garden Salon provides the workspace in an organic one — a garden you tend, a salon where minds gather. The CRDT shared state means agents and humans edit the same document simultaneously, coordinating through the artifact rather than through explicit messages. This stigmergic approach makes collaboration feel natural rather than transactional.
