Lowbeer addresses a practical problem for anyone running AI agents on a laptop: background processes drain the battery fast. It monitors power state and workload priority, throttling low-priority compute when on battery while preserving responsiveness for interactive work. The name fits — like its namesake, it watches everything and acts only when intervention is warranted.
