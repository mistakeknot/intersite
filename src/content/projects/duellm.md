DueLLM treats adversarial competition as a lens for model evaluation. By placing language models in structured dueling scenarios with Flow-Lenia dynamics as the interaction substrate, it surfaces capabilities and weaknesses that benchmark suites miss. Static evals measure what a model can do in isolation; DueLLM measures what happens when two models push back against each other in real time.
