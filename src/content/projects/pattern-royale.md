Pattern Royale turns cellular automata from a mathematical curiosity into a competitive game. Players design rule sets and deploy them onto a persistent shared grid where patterns battle for territory through local interactions alone. The result is emergent strategy — no player can predict exactly how their pattern will behave when it encounters others, and the most interesting outcomes arise from rule interactions nobody anticipated.
