Nartopo explores the idea that narrative structure has more in common with topology than with timelines. Stories branch, merge, loop, and fold back on themselves. By representing these structures as graphs with well-defined traversal rules, Nartopo enables generative storytelling where the shape of the narrative is itself a design parameter.
