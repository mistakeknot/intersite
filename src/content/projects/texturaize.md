Texturaize solves the voice problem that every AI-assisted writing workflow hits: the output sounds like a language model, not like you. It ingests writing samples, builds a voice profile, and applies that profile during editorial review. Within Sylveste, it serves as a pipeline gate — no AI-generated text reaches publication without passing through Texturaize and a human reviewer.
